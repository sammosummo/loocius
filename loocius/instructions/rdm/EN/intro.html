Random-dot motion (RDM)

In this experiment, you will see dots moving in various directions across the screen. Some dots with be moving coherently to either the left or right. Your task is to judge this direction, by pressing the left or right arrow keys on your keyboard.

Trials are completed in blocks of 99 seconds. During a block, you will also see a countdown timer and a scoreboard. You should try to achieve the highest score you can before the timer runs out and a new block starts.
